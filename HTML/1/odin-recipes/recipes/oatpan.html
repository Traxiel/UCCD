How to make Oatmeal Pancake

Description

The recipe of this Pancake is so simple.
The batter with oats and buttermilk is prepared in a food processor or blender, so it's ready to cook in minutes. Serve with syrup and butter or a dollop of applesauce.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup quick cooking oats
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine buttermilk, flour, oats, egg, oil, sugar, vanilla, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a blender; pur√©e until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour 1/4 cupfuls batter onto the hot griddle.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, 1 to 2 minutes.
  6. Flip and cook until browned on the other side, 1 to 2 minutes more.
  7. Serve immediately.
